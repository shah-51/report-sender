Report Sender

Posts a Google Sheet report to a WhatsApp group — on demand or on a schedule. Runs on your own computer. No server, no cost.

Download
Finding the latest version…
Windows may warn “unknown publisher” → click More info → Run anyway (the app is safe, just not code-signed).
On Mac, after opening the .dmg and dragging the app to Applications, right-click the app → Open the first time.

Set it up (about 5 minutes)

  1. Install & open Report Sender.
  2. Connect Google — log into the account that can see your sheet, then click “I’ve logged in”.
  3. Connect WhatsApp — scan the QR with your phone (WhatsApp → Linked devices).
  4. + Add report — paste your Google Sheet URL, the cell range (e.g. B64:C90), and the exact WhatsApp group name. Hit Send now to test.
  5. 🕒 Schedule on the report to pick how often, what time, and your time zone.

Closing the window keeps it running in the system tray so schedules fire. Turn on “Start automatically at login”. The app updates itself when a new version ships — you only download once.

Is it private?

Yes. There’s no server — nothing is sent to anyone. Your WhatsApp session, Google login, settings, and the report image all stay on your own computer. The only traffic is to your Google account (to fetch your sheet) and your WhatsApp (to post to your group). You can disconnect either at any time.
